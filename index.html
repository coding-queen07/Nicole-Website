About Me

Hello!

My name is Nicole, and I am from Montclair, New Jersey. I am a student journalist at The George Washington University, with a minor in psychological & brain sciences. I have always had a strong curiousity about the people and world around me. Most recently, I have been writing stories that explore the issues in DC Public Schools, as well as writing data-based stories.

Outside of class, I like to keep active in the DC community. Some of the things I participate in include:

  • Working at the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History, where I help facilitate labs for students on field trips and organize various collections.
  • Working as the Academic Development Director for the Gamma Kappa chapter of Kappa Alpha Theta.
  • Being the Events Director for my school's chapter on It's On Us, a club that works to combat sexual assault and violence on campus.
  • Leading and matching for the mentorship program for the prestigious GW Women's Leadership Program.
  • Simply exploring DC from the food to its museums and historical sites!
